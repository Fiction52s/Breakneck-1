Gorilla
Enemy
gorilla
Strong enemy that throws a large energy wall at you. You can block the wall,
and if you catch it in a time slow bubble, it becomes hittable and also
loses its ability to hurt you.

Specter
Enemy
specter
Shoots bullets that are almost impossible to dodge. They can be blocked, or
catching the bullet in a time slow bubble will cause it to instantly dissipate.

Laser Wolf
Enemy
laserwolf
Shoots a long laser that can bounce off walls. The laser can be made more
manageable by catching it in a time slow bubble.

Dragon
Enemy
dragon
Terror of the skies. Flies around and occassionally dives at you and then
resumes flying.

Skunk
Enemy
skunk
Explodes if you get too close! Be careful when interacting with this enemy.
You must either hit it before the explosion, or bait it out and attack after.

Free Flight Booster
Enemy
freeflightbooster
If you touch this, it gives you the power to fly freely for 5 seconds. Simply
hold the direction you want to go to drift that way.

Swing Launcher
Enemy
swinglauncher
Swings you around. Jump off when ready!

Red Wire Juggler
Enemy
redwirejuggler
Can only be hit with the red wire! Flies off in the direction it was hit,
but moves in a an arc due to gravity.

Blue Wire Juggler
Enemy
bluewirejuggler
Can only be hit with the blue wire! Flies off in the direction it was hit,
but moves in a an arc due to gravity.

Free Flight Water
Water
WATER_FREEFLIGHT
Entering this water puts you into a free flight state. Press any direction to
accelerate in that direction.

Inverted Inputs Water
Water
WATER_INVERTEDINPUTS
While within this water, your left/right and up/down inputs are reversed. Upon 
entering or exiting the water, your directional inputs are frozen for a short
time to allow you to switch to inverted or normal controls smoothly without
losing speed.

Fade Rail
Rail
FADE
Disappears when you touch it. Reappears soon after.

Wire Only Rail
Rail
WIREONLY
This rail type can only be touched with wires. You will pass through it.

Wire Blocking Rail
Rail
WIREBLOCKING
You cannot interact with this rail in any way, but it blocks wires that are shot
at it.

Boost Grass
Grass
BOOST
Instantly accelerates you to a high speed when touched.

Anti-Wire Grass
Grass
ANTIWIRE
If you a shoot a wire and it hits this grass, the wire will be cancelled.

Magi S-Kin
Skin
SKIN_MAGI
