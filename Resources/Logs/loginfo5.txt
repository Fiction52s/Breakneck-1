Swarm
Enemy
swarm
A swarm colony that holds 5 smaller enemies and launches them at you if you get close. The smaller
enemies are on a timer and will die after a few seconds. It will continue to generate enemies 
until you kill it.

Lizard
Enemy
lizard
Chases you on the ground while sometimes producing energy shocks which travel along the terrain.

Parrot
Enemy
parrot
Flying enemy that chases you but stays at a distance where it can lob bullets onto you.

Homing Launcher
Enemy
hominglauncher
Launches you in a direction of your choice. During the launch, holding the attack
button will cause you to do an attack and home in on enemies.

Time Slow Booster
Enemy
timebooster
If you touch this, every piece of the game moves in slowmotion except
for you for 5 seconds.

Homing Booster
Enemy
homingbooster
If you touch this, for 5 seconds your regular sword attacks are replaced with
an attack that will make you home in on enemies. Can be held to attack continuously
until the 5 seconds are up.

Anti-Timeslow Booster
Enemy
antitimeslowbooster
If you touch this, for 5 seconds you will not be effected by timeslow effects at
all.

Hungry Comboer
Enemy
hungrycomboer
Once hit, it will chase down and eat any enemies in the area.
Also has a limited verison which will reset after a certain amount
of enemies killed.

Relative Comboer
Enemy
relativecomboer
Can be hit into other enemies. After it travels a short distance, it will lock its position
relative to Kin and destroy any enemies it touches. Also has a limited version.

Relative Detach Comboer
Enemy
relativecomboerdetach
Can be hit into other enemies. After it travels a short distance, it will lock its position
relative to Kin. If it kills an enemy, it will unlock and remain stationary until hit again.

Homing Detach Comboer
Enemy
relativecomboerdetach
Can be hit into other enemies. After it travels a short distance, it will lock its position
relative to Kin. If it kills an enemy, it will unlock and remain stationary until hit again.

Time Slow Water
Water
WATER_TIMESLOW
While within this water, your actions are performed slower, and you move slower. You do have
infinitely refreshing air options, however.

Poison Water
Water
WATER_POISON
While within this water, your health drains at a very fast rate. You also have infinitely
refreshing air options and will accelerate slightly in any direction that you move.

Locked Rail

Touching this rail forces you to stay on it until you reach the end. You can also
pass through terrain completely while riding on it.

AntiTimeslow Rail
Rail

Works similarly to a Grind Rail, except that while on it, you are immune to timeslow effects.
