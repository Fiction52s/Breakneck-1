Gravity Juggler
Enemy
downgravityjuggler
Can be hit into other enemies to deal damage. Goes a different
direction based on which attack you hit it with. Has a limited
version where it will reset after a certain number of hits.

Reverse Gravity Juggler
Enemy
upgravityjuggler
Can be hit into other enemies to deal damage. Goes a different
direction based on which attack you hit it with. Falls up instead of down.
Has a limited version where it will reset after a certain number of hits.

Bat
Enemy
bat
Shoots bullets at you machine gun style. Will move away from you if you get close,
and return to its normal position if you give it enough space.

Lob Turret
Enemy
lobturret
Shoots bullets in a lob pattern that fall with gravity. Has a 3 bullet cycle of angles
to shoot at. Also has a reverse gravity version.

Poison Frog
Enemy
poisonfrog
Jumping enemy. If you are close to it at the peak of its jump, it will doublejump.
It can also climb walls. Also has a reverse gravity version.

Stag Rhino
Enemy
stagbeetle
Heavy enemy that rushes you on the ground. Higher leveled versions have a shield.

Gravity Faller
Enemy
gravityfaller
Loops between falling down and falling up.

Gravity Increaser
Enemy
gravityincreaser
If you touch this, you'll be granted increased gravity for the next 5 seconds.

Gravity Decreaser
Enemy
gravitydecreaser
If you touch this, you'll be granted decreased gravity for the next 5 seconds.

Glide Launcher
Enemy
glidespring
Launches you in a set direction with a set velocity, but you can use Up/Down to turn
and aim yourself in the air.

Glide Water
Water
WATER_GLIDE
Forces you into a state of gliding. Use up/down to aim yourself. Glide consistently
at the speed you entered the water.

Low Gravity Water
Water
WATER_LOWGRAV
Decreases the effect of gravity.

High Gravity Water
Water
WATER_HEAVYGRAV
Increases the effect of gravity.

Buoyancy Water
Water
WATER_BUOYANCY
Causes you to rise to the surface whenever you are in it. Upon entering, your airdash will
be restored if you have already used it.

Floor Rail
Rail
FLOOR
Rail that can only be used as a floor. You can pass through it from below. Also pressing
straight down will cause you to drop through.

Ceiling Rail
Rail
CEILING
Rail that can only be used as a ceiling. You will pass it through it from above.
You are always able to walk on the ceiling for this rail, even if you have no powers.

Gravity Reverse Grass
Grass
GRAVREVERSE
This grass allows you to walk on the ceiling by touching any ceiling that its on.
